Chao Wang 王超

Ph.D. Candidate

Zhejiang University

Chao Wang is currently a fourth-year PhD. Candidate in the College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University. His supervisor is Prof. Feng Lin. His research interests include Wireless Sensing, Machine/Deep Learning, and Mobile Computing. He received his B.E. degree from the College of Information Science and Electronic Engineering at Zhejiang University in 2019.
